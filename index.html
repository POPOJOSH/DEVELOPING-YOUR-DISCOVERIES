DEVELOPING YOUR DISCOVERIES

DEVELOPING YOUR DISCOVERIES is not intended by the author to be an historical study of materials on discoveries. Rather, the book is intended to serve as a mysterious key, tool, and weapon to weary and discouraged hearts, challenging them to arise to the task of developing their discoveries, as the Lord has commanded us to occupy till he comes.


DEVELOPING YOUR DISCOVERIES is also further intended to fan and stoke the embers of purpose fulfilment in the hearts of those who are already on this lonely path of pursuit of the genuine glory of God, and becoming all that the divinity has destined for humanity here on earth before the ultimate transition. This book will hopefully awaken a genuine desire in the human heart to deepen personal search and study on how to “develop all the deposited virtues (discoveries) in one's life!


Every line starts with a dot, and every circle starts with a point. There would not be an end without a beginning. Such is the life of man. There would not be fulfilment of purpose, if there is no purpose in the first place.


There would not be manifestation of whatsoever great things man has achieved or would still achieve, if there has not been imagination of such things first. Though it's manifestation that attracts the applause of men and worth celebrating, but there wouldn't have been such celebration without imagination. And manifestation is not attained by a sudden flight; it's only actualized through the right processes. It's the following of the due processes that will bring the demanded products to a reality and manifestation. Such is the discoveries of man, and the development thereof.


It's not enough to have a good and beautiful pictures snapped, and remain in the camera's film, as negative. It is far more important, and great to have them washed, and printed, to be seen. More so, it is not enough to have the plan of a good mansion building, to be drawn on a paper, it's more beautiful, great and important, though demanding, to have it physically built, erected, and developed on the land, to be seen as a full building. But, this erecting, building, and developing is not achieved suddenly. It must follow the due processes to attain the required and desired expectation, which is the mansion. So also is the DEVELOPING OF YOUR DISCOVERIES.



In the following pages of this book are the mysteries, and keys to help you develop all your discoveries, all to the glory of God, and fulfill the purpose of your being, by taking your dream from the realm of imagination to the realm of manifestation, and from obscurity to limelight.


Let's catch the revival fire, by developing our discoveries, and work tirelessly for our King and His Kingdom, for “…THE NIGHT COMETH WHEN NO MAN CAN WORK.” {John.9:4}


It is my heartfelt prayer for you that as you read this book, that it will genuinely set your heart ablaze for God like never before. As your life catches the revival fire of God to developing your discoveries, may your life burn with great passion and zeal for God. May you encounter the mighty hands of God, for great and positive radical change, as you read, and study the words in the pages of this book. Happy reading! See you at the Top!


SAMSON OLUWAMUYIWA O. AJEWOLE
